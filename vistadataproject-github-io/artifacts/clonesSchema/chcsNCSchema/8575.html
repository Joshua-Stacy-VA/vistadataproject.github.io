description
The MCP Entitlement Discrepancy Codes indicate that the basis for a patient's eligibility within the DOD medical health care system has changed since the time that the patient was originally enrolled/empaneled into managed care. An example would be a patient who was enrolled as an active duty member but who has now retired. This code indicates to the site the nature of the entitlement change and signals that the patient should be disenrolled and possibly re-enrolled, if appropriate.
