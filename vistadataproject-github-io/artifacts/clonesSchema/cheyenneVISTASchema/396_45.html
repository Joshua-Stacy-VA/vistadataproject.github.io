description
This file contains contacts for external Contractors (or Companies) who are authorized to perform 2507 exams for VA patients at their respective clinic/hospital. Note: SFTP information will be maintained and referenced from the central CLAIMS VistA system, denoted by the REMOTE CONTRACTOR Field #3. SFTP information added locally will be ignored by the demTRAN application.
