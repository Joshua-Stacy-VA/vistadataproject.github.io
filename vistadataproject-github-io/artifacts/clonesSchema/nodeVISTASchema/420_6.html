description
This file contains FPDS codes for fiscal years 88 & 89. These FPDS codes are used to track the types of businesses from which goods are being procured, in order to do reporting to Central Office. All codes with internal entry #'s below 100 are for fiscal year 88. All codes with internal entry #'s above 100 are for fiscal year 89.
