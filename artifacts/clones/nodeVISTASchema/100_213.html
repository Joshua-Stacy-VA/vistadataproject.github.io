This field will determine how the Autolinks for Providers will work. If the field is set to; "P" - Primary: The autolink will add patients to the list that have the pointed to provider assigned to them thru the MAS options as PROVIDER. "A" - Attending: The autolink will add patients to the list that have the pointed to provider assigned to them thru the MAS options as ATTENDING PHYSICIAN. "B" - Both: The autolink will add patients to the list that have the pointed to provider assigned to them thru the MAS options as 'PROVIDER' and those patients that have the pointed to provider assigned to them as ATTENDING PHYSICIAN.
