XUMFAn Institution has a STATUS (#11) of either LOCAL or NATIONAL. Inactive facilities are flagged as such by the field INACTIVE FACILITY FLAG (#101). National entries are facilities that have a STATION NUMBER (#99) approved by Information Management Service (045A4). National entries are maintained by the Master File Server located on FORUM. Local entries are facilities that are entered locally. Local entries no not have a STATION NUMBER (#99). (Only offically approved station numbers are allowed in the STATION NUMBER (#99) field.) All national entries will have a STATUS (#11) of NATIONAL. Do not edit the STATUS (#11) of a national entry. Do not set a LOCAL entry to a STATUS (#11) of NATIONAL.
