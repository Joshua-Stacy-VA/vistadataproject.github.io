description
This file contains the VACO-approved reasons a room-bed or ward may be placed out-of-service. These entries must not be altered in any way. The internal entry numbers and the entries themselves have been added under the direction of MAS VACO and are the only approved reasons for placing wards and beds out-of-service. Altering of this data in any way can have severe and negative impacts on the operations of many DHCP packages.
