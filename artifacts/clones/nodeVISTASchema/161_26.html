description
Contains the entry of veterans whose master record information has changed. The updated information must be sent to the Centralized Fee system in Austin for the updating of their master veteran records. Per VHA Directive 10-93-142, this file definition should not be modified.

This field is used as a flag and relates to outpatient medical reimbursements for a veteran with only a short term authorization.
