The data in this file will be extremely volatile, and any data older than a day will be removed automatically. The load-balancer uses the data in this file in two ways: - when the first image of a new study is being processed, the load-balancer will calculate the designated recipient for that study, based on the routing rule in question - when subsequent images of that study are being processed the load-balancer will ensure that they are transmitted to the same destination.
