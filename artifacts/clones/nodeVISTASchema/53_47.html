This file holds the abbreviations used when entering the Infusion Rate (#.08) field in the IV (#100) multiple of the PHARMACY PATIENT (#55) file, and the INFUSION RATE (#59) field in the NON-VERIFIED ORDERS (#53.1) file. Each record holds an expansion of the abbreviation which replaces the abbrevation in the Infusion Rate at the time the IV order is created.
