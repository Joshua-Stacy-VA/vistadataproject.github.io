DEFINES THE LOAD/WORK LIST TO BE MADE FROM THE ACCESSION LIST The ^LRO(68.2,LRINST,10,LRPROF,1,"AO",TEST) = ORDER X-ref is setup by BUILD^LRLLP4 and holds the order to print the tests on the load/work list.
