description
This file contains a table of military and government services, e.g. AIR FORCE, FOREIGN MILITARY, and U.S. PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE. In addition, it contains multi-service entries such as ARMY/AIR FORCE used by the CHCS system to denote multi-service relations.
