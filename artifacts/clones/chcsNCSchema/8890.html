description
This file will define site parameters for divisions in QA. Each division will have unique timing requirements related to tracking and following up events and occurrence reviews. These may be MTF policy or QA departmental policy.

.01division(+)0;1POINTER40.8BThis field contains the division to which the site parameters apply. Each division has individual parameters as to whom QAN Incidents are forwarded, how far in the past meetings may be scheduled, and the point at which events become delinquent.
1send qa notice to0;2POINTER3FQA User to whom Ancillary Incidents are Forwarded - The user name which is entered into this field defines the person who receives a mail bulletin when ancillary incidents are forwarded from QA Nursing to Facility QA.
2occurrence screening period0;3NUMERICNumber of days when Review is considered Overdue - The number of days after a patient's discharge from an inpatient admission, within which his/her record must be batch posted as having had an occurrence screening performed (batch posted Negative Occurrence Screening). If the record has not been batch posted, it is considered delinquent and will appear on the Delinquent Occurrence Screening Report.
3scheduling past date0;4NUMERICNumber of days in past beyond which meeting may not be scheduled - The number of days, prior to the date that a user is actually entering a meeting date/time, that the system will accept as a valid entry. Scheduling meetings in the past is quite often necessary to allow recording of review committee activities for impromptu or unscheduled meetings.
