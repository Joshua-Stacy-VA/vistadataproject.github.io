.01key group0;1FREE TEXTBThis field is used for bulk assignment of security keys when a site or specific application is being implemented. Bulk assignment for users who have filemanager access identifying them as system managers will be prompted for key group assignments of keys. This means a key may be flagged as being both a 'Pharmacist' and 'Pharmacy tech' since both groups should hold this key. The keys to be assigned will be decided by the flags in this field. The site manager may then merely enter "Pharmacist" as the group and all keys being flagged as keys that should be held by a pharmacist will be assigned to the user being assigned as a holder and the keys will not need to be listed one by one.
