.01billable service0;1FREE TEXTBThe Billable Service Sub-file will be used for itemized billing. Itemized billing requires additional information not needed previous when DD7A billing was performed by MEPRS code. With the implementation of Itemized Billing, charges will be based on sales code, service category, and date of service. The rate used will be dependent on the type of service. Multiple rate files have been provided to support this function.
