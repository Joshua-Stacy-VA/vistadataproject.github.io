There is one Payer Type associated with each Coverage Type. The Payer Type is the link between the policy in the OHI file, and the address in the Health Insurance Carrier (HIC) file where the claims associated with specific charges should be sent. The Payer Types are as follows Professional, Institutional, and Both (Professional and Institutional).
