description
This file is a standard DOD (Group 1) file which is sent fully populated to the sites, with annual updates provided. A government billing calculation factor for billing Outpatient International Military Education and Training (IMO), and Interagency Rate (IOR), will be applied to the line item charges calculated for outpatient medical and ancillary services. All Rate Tables, except for Dental Procedures, are understood to be the Full Outpatient Rate, which will not require a government billing calculation factor to be applied. A government billing calculation factor will not be applied to Dental procedures coded with HCPCS Level II "D" Codes, as the Dental Rate table has specific rates calculated for IMO, IOR and the Full Outpatient Rate (FOR).
