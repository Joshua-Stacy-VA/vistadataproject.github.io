.13OVERALL COSIGN STATUS0;13SET OF CODES0:countersignature is not required
1:countersignature required
2:completed by countersigning
This is the overall status of the consult result. Consults may be defined as requiring countersignature by the consulting provider. This is defined in the consulting provider's preferences file. A countersigning HCP may be a provider with an equal or greater signature class (Class 1 may sign for another Class 1 provider). Once the consult has been verified by the consulting HCP the consult will display in the consult listing, under Co-sign, for the countersigning HCP. Once countersigned the consult will be completed. Consults may also be appended by a provider requiring countersignature. These consults will then display in the consult listing of the countersigning HCP. Until they are countersigned, the consult will display/print as 'unverified'.
