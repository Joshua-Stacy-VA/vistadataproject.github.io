description
This file contains the Health Insurance Carrier (HIC) Status Codes. The HIC Status Codes are applied to each Health Insurance Carrier. The codes indicate the status of the carrier, in terms of where the carrier entry stands in the verification process. Newly entered carriers will always have a HIC Status of "Temporary" until they are verified by the Verification Point of Contact (VPOC) for the DEERS HIC database.
