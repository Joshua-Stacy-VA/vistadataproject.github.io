2:RECEIVED
3:RELEASED

PENDING means the SADR task has triggered an HL7 message to send data to CCE. RECEIVED means CCE has sent data back and the data has been stored. RELEASED means CCE will not modify the encounter data (CCE users did not have time). COMPLETE means the SADR task has processed the record without error and retransmitted it. UNCERTIFIED means the same thing as RELEASED only the record was for a billable encounter and was released by the 90-day sweep instead of the 28-day sweep for non-billable encounters.
