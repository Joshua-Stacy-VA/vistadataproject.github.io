.02SELECTION CHARACTER(+)0;2FREE TEXTSELThe character a user is allowed to type to 'jump' directly to an action on a bar and must therefore be unique for the bar. This character will be Capitalized and Highlighted on the bar.
.03DISPLAY ORDER(+)0;3NUMERICORDERThis expresses the relative order of this item on the bar. The elements are presented in Numeric order by this field's value. The value of this field must be unique within a bar. Number in increments of 5 or 10 to facilitate easier insertion of new items on the bar. (ie. 5, 10, 15, etc.) This is also the value used to control the position of the slider on the bar both by bar operations and by the default position.
.04SPACES AFTER ITEM0;4NUMERICThis is the number of spaces which will be included after this item on a bar. If the item is the last one on the bar, this field is ignored. If this field is NULL, the value of three is assumed.
