7DATE OF LAST PATIENT ACTIVITY0;7DATE-TIMEThis field contains the date of the patient's last treatment activity and is used to determine the eligibility of a record for retirement. Examples of last patient activity includes, last prescription filled, last clinic appointment kept by the patient, last x-ray taken. Enter the date you want the system to begin searching for records which are eligible for retirement or transfer. When retiring records enter a date based on the retention period of the medical record type for your branch of the service.
