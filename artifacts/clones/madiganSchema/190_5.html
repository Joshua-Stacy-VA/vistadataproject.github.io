RECORD TRANSFER-RETIRE LIST
number
190.5
location
^RTV(190.5,
description
The Record Transfer-Retire List contains lists of records which have been generated by CHCS to assist in the process of retiring or transferring (between MTFs or within an MTF) medical treatment records.
