description
The Patients of Interest file stores patients identified by offboards systems as being of interest in current or ongoing studies being conducted at the facility. Once added to the file, a entry may be active or inactive. Active entries that are identified for a particular offboard are used in selectively routing broadcast messages to that offboard system.
