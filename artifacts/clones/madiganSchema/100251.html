The format of the field is "NX", where "N" is the numeric value of the range and "X" is the date/time indicator (i.e., "s" indicates seconds, "m" indicates minutes, "h" indicates hours, "D" indicates days, "M" indicates months, and "Y" indicates years. For example, "10m" indicates a MEASUREMENT PERIOD of 10 minutes, and "10Y" indicates a MEASUREMENT PERIOD of 10 years.
