This field determines the level of signature authority for the provider. A nurse level authority can write and activate orders, but these orders must be also signed by an HCP. The PROVIDER REQUIRING COUNTERSIGNATURE is similar to the nurse in the sense that a countersignature is always needed. The HCP level is the standard physician authority and is the highest level needed for all orders except those special procedures requiring a countersignature. The COUNTERSIGNATURE HCP is a generic countersignature authority which could be used to countersign all orders requiring countersignature authority except those requiring countersignature keys.
