This file is used to store intervention types. Intervention types are actually reasons that a pharmacist had to stop filling a prescription and contact a provider to either change the prescription or get clarification of it. Examples of intervention types may include things such as: CRITICAL DRUG INTERACTION, ALLERGY, INCORRECT DOSE, etc.
