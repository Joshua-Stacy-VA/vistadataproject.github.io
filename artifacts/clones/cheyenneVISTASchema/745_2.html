description
This file contains a list of reasons/issues for contacts. Some reasons are called headings and may not be edited or added to by the user. Headings from version 1.0 were inactivated with the installation of version 2.0. The new headings are as follows: Staff Courtesy, Access/Timeliness, One Provider, Decisions/Preference, Emotional Needs Coordination of Care, Patient Education, Family Involvement, Physical Comfort, Transitions, Risk Management Complaints, Medical Records Issues, Eligibility Issues, Environmental Issues, Regulation Issues, Requests for Information and Compliments. Only Nationally exported Issues Codes can be used.
