description
This file contains the Status History of an ROI instance. It is the way to track the historical processing of a request. The following are the accepted statuses and dispositions: STATUS 'O' FOR OPEN 'C-G' FOR CLOSED GRANTED 'C-P' FOR CLOSED PARTIAL 'C-PR' FOR CLOSED/PENDING PAYMENT RELEASED 'C-D' FOR CLOSED DENIED 'P' FOR PENDING 'P-C' FOR PENDING CLARIFICATION 'P-P' FOR PENDING PAYMENT (OBSOLETED IN V 7.1) 'P-H' FOR PENDING PAYMENT HELD 'N-NR' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE NO RECORDS 'N-RF' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE REFERRAL 'N-ND' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE NOT DESCRIBED 'N-NV' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE NOT VA RECORD 'N-RC' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE OTHER (OBSOLETED IN V 7.1) 'N-NF' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE FEE RELATED 'N-NU' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE DUPLICATE REQUEST 'N-NP' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE NOT PROPER FOIA 'N-MS' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE MEDICALLY SENSITIVE 'N-PD' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE PATIENT DIED BEFORE COMPLETION 'N-PA' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE PUBLICLY AVAILABLE 'N-G' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE GLOMAR 'N-SL' FOR OTHER NONDISCLOSURE SUBSUMED BY LITIGATION 'A-RV' FOR APPEALED REVERSED 'A-PR' FOR APPEALED PARTIAL REVERSED 'X' FOR CANCELLED 'E' FOR ERROR 'A' FOR APPEALED
