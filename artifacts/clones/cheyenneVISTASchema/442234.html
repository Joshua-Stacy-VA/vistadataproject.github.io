description
Tracking system for the Chaplain Serivce of Spiritual Assessments and Care Plans of inpatients and outpatients at the Cheyenne VA Medical Center at the request of Chaplain Carr. INPATIENTS Initial Assessment should be within 24 hours of admission in Hospice and ICU Wards. All other admission assessments should be within 48 hours. Follow-up assessments should be every 14 days with the exception of CLC patients, who should have assessments every 90 days. OUTPATIENTS Home Base Primary Care, Hospice, and Palliative Care patients should be assessed within 48 hours and have reassessments every 90 days. Substance Abuse patients should be assessed within 14 days of enrollment and have reassessments every 90 days.
