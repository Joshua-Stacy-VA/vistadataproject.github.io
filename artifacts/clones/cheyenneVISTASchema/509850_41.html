.01allowable modifiers0;1FREE TEXTBThe ALLOWABLE MODIFIERS field contains the letter modifiers for a CPT procedure code. CPT-4 codes for Audiology and Speech Pathology may not adequately describe the scope or variety of procedures seen by audiologists and speech pathologists. Recognizing this deficiency, modifiers have been developed for certain codes to clarify procedures or to provide codes for procedures where no codes now exist. New modifiers should only be added at the direction of the Director, Audiology and Speech Pathology Service (VAHQ).
.02description0;2FREE TEXTThe DESCRIPTION field contains the descriptor for the modifier of the CPT-4 procedure code. This descriptor should only be edited at the direction of the Director, Audiology and Speech Pathology Service (VAHQ).
