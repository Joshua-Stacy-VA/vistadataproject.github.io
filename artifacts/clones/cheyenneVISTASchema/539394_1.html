description
This Inpatient Evaluation Center File contains the results of selected medical tests acquired for the project's study patients, currently including vitals measurements. It relies on a system of defined study windows in the data acquisition and files population processes. The source for these defined windows is the IPEC Study Windows File (#539394.2). For the purposes of the project, EVERY critical care or acute care episode (depending on the study) is selected for scrutiny. Selected medical values, in particular, are grouped and evaluated as they occur within these windows. The window formation uses the unit or ward entry date /time and its starting point of reference. The first window spans the time width of 192 hours (8 days) starting exactly 168 hours (7 days) prior to unit entry and extending to 24 hours (1 day) following unit entry. Subsequent days of critical care are tracked in contiguous 24 hours windows. The Unit Exit Date is included so that one last, overlapping 24 hours window is included in the model. * Window Model * ========================================================================= Unit 1st 2nd 3rd Unit Entry Unit Unit Unit Exit D/T Window Window Win D/T V V V V V {-------- 192h -------}*{--- 24h ----}*{-- 24h --}*{--- 24h --} ========================================================================= NOTE: The exceptionally wide first window is an accommodation for the study of critical care occurrences involving surgical procedures occurring in the operating room setting. All other non-OR cases will use a survey window 48 hours in width, 24 hours before and after unit entry during the home site processing of data.
