C A L E N D A R v3.0 September, 1993 - by - Roger Ackerman Calendar is a personal productivity tool. With it you enter and maintain entries into a personal calendar, todo list, or store general information in electronic 'index cards'. Calendar 'groups' can also be setup to track events and todo items shared by the group, like teleconferences, QA reports, assignments, etc. Calendar users who belong to these groups can optionally have group events and todo information displayed along with their personal calendars and todo lists. This version also includes a form of group scheduling, where a user can query for common free time among calendar users, and can then optionally send individual calendar events to each user. Calendar features include: Day, Week, and Month displays Edit, View, List, and Print Calendar or ToDo entries Six month planner calendar display Enter/Edit Index Cards Send a Calendar notice to another user Group Availability the Forwarding of entries to other users
