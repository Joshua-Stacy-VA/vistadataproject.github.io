3status0;4SET OF CODES0:TRANSMITTED
1:DISPENSED
2:RETRANSMITTED
3:NOT DISPENSED
The current status of the prescription fill for the Consolidated Mail Outpatient Pharmacy. A status of Transmitted means that the Rx has been transmitted to the CMOP for processing. A status of Dispensed means that the Rx has been filled and mailed by the CMOP. A status of Retransmitted means that the original transmission with this Rx had to be retransmitted to the CMOP. A status of Not Dispensed means that the vendor system was not able to fill the Rx. If the status is Not Dispensed, the reason will be in the Reason field.
