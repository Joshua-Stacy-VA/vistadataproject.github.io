Enter one or more teaching codes for this exam. Teaching codes may be entered in two ways. If you know all the digits of the teaching code, then enter them and press . If you want to build a teaching code, then begin your selection process by entering one of the numbers listed above followed by two question marks. The CHCS accepts the number(s) you entered and display a list of selections at the next level down. NOTE: If you enter an incorrect number, then enter a caret (^) and press . For example, if you begin by entering 1?? or 12??, then the CHCS displays the codes under the SKULL AND CONTENTS or BASE OF SKULL categories, respectively, and allow you to select the next digit(s). As you select additional number(s) to build the teaching code, the CHCS continues to display subsequent lists of codes.
