description
This file indicates the total number of times within a 24-hour period that an order will be carried out, designating the specific times, the frequency, and the duration. The fields designate the hospital location, its order timing schedule, and its default timing schedule.

Indicate whether this entry will be used to designate: 1) the specific times that the order will be carried out (i.e., QID would indicate specific timing for the order), 2) the inter-day frequency (i.e., QOD would indicate the frequency of the order as every other day), 3) the duration of the order (i.e, F10D would indicate a duration of 10 days).
