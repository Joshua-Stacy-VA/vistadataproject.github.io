Spooling requirement of the device (NOTE: Not to be confused with a SPOOL DOCUMENT.) Devices that require two-way communication (such as a LSI device) must be set as "NOT SPOOLABLE." When a device is "SPOOLABLE" users will be able to use the Reprint Report and Query Print Status secondary menu options on print jobs sent to this device. Devices that are "SPOOL ONLY" are devices that require special drivers within the device handler, such as TCP: connected printers.
