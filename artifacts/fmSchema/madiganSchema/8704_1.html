description
This file maintains the summary totals of work accomplished by Cytotechnologists within the Cytology department. Also tracked are the Cytotech's REVIEWER and the number of 'agreements' of diagnostic screening. A summary report is available of these statistics by accessing the menu option "Cytology Quality Control (CQ)" found under the Quality Control Menu.
