.01DAY(S)0;1FREE TEXTBUsed to describe the days of a week that a print option will be restricted. Valid entries are M for Monday, TU for Tuesday, W for Wednesday. TH for Thursday, F for Friday, SA for Saturday, SU for Sunday and H for Holidays. Day ranges start with SUnday and end with SAturday. Valid forms fo entry are: M-f is Monday thru Friday SA,SU is Saturday and Sunday M,W,F,H is Monday, Wednesday, Friday and Holidays Holidays are determined by entries in the HOLIDAY file (#40.5). NOTE: If one or more days are defined and no time is defined, users will not be restricted from queueing, they will however be forced to queue in those applications that are integrated with this functionality.
.02TIME(S)0;2FREE TEXTThis field is used to describe times to prohibit queuing of print options. Valid entries are military times in the following format: 0800-0900 is 0800 to 0900 0800-0900,1300-1400 is 0800 to 0900 and 1300 to 1400
