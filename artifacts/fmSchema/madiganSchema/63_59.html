A:AMENDED/UNIT STATUS UPDATED
This field indicates the current status of the accession. DBSS will transmit only certified blood bank results to CHCS. Even though the results are certified, the accession will have a status of Intermediate until the last certified result and the last unit status is transmitted to CHCS for this accession. At this time, the accession status will be Final. Amending an individual blood bank test result will not change the accession status to amended. The accession status will be Amended only if a change is made to the unit status after the accession has a status of Final.
