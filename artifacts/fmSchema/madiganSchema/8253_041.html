When this flag is set, the severity level for this interaction could be strength dependent. Interactions are assigned severity levels--Moderate, Severe, or Contraindicated. For certain interactions the severity level is dependent on the strength of the medication. For example, a 10mg tablet could result in a moderate interaction against another drug while a 20mg or greater strength tablet could result in a severe or contraindicated severity level for the same interaction.
