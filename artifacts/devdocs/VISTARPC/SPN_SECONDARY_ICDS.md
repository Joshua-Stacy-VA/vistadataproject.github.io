# SPN SECONDARY ICDS

The "Secondary Conditions" scroll box display on the Impairments Tab will be populated with ICD-9 diagnostic, from the patient's beginning date tothe current date.  But it excludes the 806, 952, 344, & 907 ICD-9 series.
