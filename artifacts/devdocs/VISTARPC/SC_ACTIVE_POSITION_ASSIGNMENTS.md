# SC ACTIVE POSITION ASSIGNMENTS

This RPC is used to obtain the active positions associated with a patientteam assignment.

--- | ---
Label | ACTPOS
MUMPS Implementation | [SCUTBK4](http://code.osehra.org/dox/Routine_SCUTBK4_source.html)
