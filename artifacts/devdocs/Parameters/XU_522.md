# XU522

Enter Y (YES) to disable old-style CAPRI logins (default).Enter E (ERROR) to disable old-style CAPRI logins and trap attempts.Enter N (NO) to leave old-style CAPRI logins enabled.Enter L (DEBUG) to leave old-style CAPRI logins enabled but trap attempts.
