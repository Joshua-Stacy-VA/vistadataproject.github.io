ORQQPX COVER SHEET REMINDERS

This parameter is cumulative, so sequence ranges should be assigned.Values are in the form of FlagTypeIEN, where Flag indicatesL:Lock, R:Remove or N:Normal, Type is a 'C' or 'R', indicatingthat # is the internal entry number of a Category or a Reminder.
