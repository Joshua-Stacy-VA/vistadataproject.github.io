# ORPF PRINT CHART COPY WHEN

Chart copies may be printed when orders are  a) released to the service  b) signed by a clinician (may be after the orders are released)This parameter determines at which point the chart copy of orders will print.  The chart copy may be printed when the order is released to the service or delayed until the order is actually signed.
