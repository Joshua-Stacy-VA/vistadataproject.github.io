1incident #1;0MULTIPLE8880.16The unique number identifying an INCIDENT. It is made up of the current year incremented by 1. For example, the first incident of 1987 would be 87-1, the second 87-2, etc. For lookup purposes, the user has the choice whether or not to enter leading zeros.
