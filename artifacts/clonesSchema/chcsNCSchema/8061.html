description
This file defines the fixed and variable rates used in MSA patient accounts for sales codes and charge categories, and the rate codes used to identify copy charges, dining hall collections surcharges and subsistence charges, and group meal sales charges. The rate history provides the dates on which rates take affect and identifies the fund distribution for charges and corresponding payments. Each rate table entry includes flags that determine the circumstances under which the rates can be used. The available rates are screened by Branch of Service as determined by the KERNEL SITE PARAMETERS File, so that only applicable rates can be 'seen' by the users.
