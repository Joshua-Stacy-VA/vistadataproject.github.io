10.02mrt queue threshold10;2NUMERIC This field determines whether transactions will be processed "while you wait" or queued to process in the background. For example, if the threshold is set to 10 and the user selects 10 or less records for a transaction (Check-in/Charge-out, etc) they will wait while the system processes them. If more than 10 records are selected, then the processing will be automatically queued to free up the terminal.
