.06policy end reason code0;6POINTER8097The Policy End Reason Code is the code that explains why the OHI policy is no longer active. Definition of Values: "E" indicates that the OHI policy is cancelled. This represents an error, and DEERS will consider the policy as having never existed. "Q" indicates that the Policy End Date is in the past. "R" indicates that the Policy End Date is today or a future date. "S" indicates that DEERS has terminated an OHI policy. The termination of a policy in DEERS is associated with a loss of the patient's eligibility. "U" indicates that there is no known Policy End Date. "D" indicates that DEERS has deactivated the insurance company that was associated with the policy. When DEERS deactivates an insurance carrier, all policies which were associated with the deactivated carrier, are also deactivated.
