.01synonym0;1FREE TEXTEEnter an abbreviation, quick code, or other familiar designation for this radiology procedure. It is recommended that frequently performed procedures be given at least one synonym. Synonyms provide a quick method to call up procedures because synonyms require fewer keystrokes that the full procedure name (for example, BE instead of BARIUM ENEMA). Synonyms also allow you to call up procedures using names that may be more familiar to you than the one entered in the NAME field. You may enter as many synonyms as necessary, but every synonym you enter must be unique to the CHCS.
