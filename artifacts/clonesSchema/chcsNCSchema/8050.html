8104service category0;25POINTER8058.5This field will indicate the service category associated with an Outpatient Account using the Itemized Billing software. This is the account level service category. Service category is also defined at the charge line item. Service Categories include LAB, RAD, PHR, and OPE, indicating Laboratory, Radiology, Pharmacy, or Outpatient Encounter (from the ADM module) respectively. A category of OTC indicates that the only charges are one-time charges. There are also 2 composite categories that are valid only at the account level. These are OPE+, to indicate that an account includes both an encounter and other charges, and CMP to indicate that the charges are a composite of multiple service categories, but that no Outpatient Encounter is associated with the account. THE ACCOUNT LEVEL SERVICE CATEGORY IS SET BY THE SYSTEM, AND MAY NOT BE ENTERED BY THE USER.
