description
This file contains the Other Health Insurance (OHI) End Reason Codes. An OHI End Reason Code is applied to each OHI policy automatically, based on either the date in the OHI End Date field, or the HIC Status Code associated with the OHI policy. The codes are as follows: "Cancelled", "Date is Certain", "Date is Estimated", "Terminated", "No Date Can Be Predicted", and "Deactivated."

This represents an error, and DEERS will consider the policy as having never existed. "Q" indicates that the Policy End Date is in the past. "R" indicates that the Policy End Date is today or a future date. "S" indicates that DEERS has terminated an OHI policy. The termination of a policy in DEERS is associated with a loss of the patient's eligibility. "U" indicates that there is no known Policy End Date. "D" indicates that DEERS has deactivated the insurance company that was associated with the policy. When DEERS deactivates an insurance carrier, all policies which were associated with the deactivated carrier, are also deactivated.
