description
The PERIOD OF SERVICE file contains 37 entries as determined by VACO MAS. Alteration of existing entries or addition of new ones will undoubtedly have a negative impact on the efficient operation of the MAS module and other modules. Updates to this file will be provided by the MAS developers by way of regular releases.
